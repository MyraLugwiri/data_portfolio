I am a computer science student at the African Leadership University, driven by my passion for data, artificial intelligence and its real-world applications. My goal is to become a successful data analyst, leveraging new and emerging technologies to help businesses make informed decisions. When I am not coding, I am a social media manager for Global Youths Projects, an NGO focused on empowering youth. I also volunteer with Engin, an organization that provides English speaking opportunities for Ukrainian students. My current interest lies in the intersection of data and finance, and how small and medium enterprises in Kenya can use their data to improve their businesses and increase sales.

I have had the privilege of getting the Mastercard Foundation Scholarship for my current undergraduate studies and also was able to successfully compete in the Loreal Brandstorm competition in a team of three and represented Rwanda in the African finals
